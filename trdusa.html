Toyota Racing Development (TRD) U.S.A. gives shape to Toyota’s performance image.  TRD is a subsidiary of Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A.   TRD designs, develops and assembles Toyota racing engines.  In addition, TRD has established an aggressive product development program, which has lead to new advancements in its line of performance aftermarket parts.  And to provide support to Toyota owners who race, TRD administers the Toyota Motorsports/TRD Contingency Award Program.  In its 15th year, this effort supports driver at the grassroots level by providing cash awards to Toyota-powered teams that compete successfully in various types of motorsports events across the United States. 

Each TRD sport parts are engineered to fit your Toyota the first time without the modification and fabrication that is typical of aftermarket brands.  TRD product quality meets or exceeds original equipment manufacturer (OEM) standards. To ensure every order is filled within a timely matter, TRD holds an extensive million-dollar inventory of parts and merchandise.
