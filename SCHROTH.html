SCHROTH street-legal harnesses are German TÜV-approved and have ECE-16.04 street-legal certification. They feature SCHROTH's unique ASM (a patented anti-submarining energy-converter mechanism system), a technology that reduces the risk of submarining - sliding beneath the lap belt. The installation/operation instructions allow for easy installation.  The Company's technical know-how and the resulting products have led to remarkable improvements in automotive safety.
