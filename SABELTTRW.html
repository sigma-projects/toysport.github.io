TRW SABELT  

TRW Sabelt is the number one manufacturer of competition harnesses in the world. Nearly every Formula One, IndyCar, and Sports Car team has used these harnesses exclusively for years. Belts are 3" wide with camlock releases, and are available in bolt-in, snap-in or wrap-around mountings. Mounting hardware kits must be purchased separately.

  • WARNING: Shoulder harnesses should be fitted as close to horizontal as possible. The connection points should always be reinforced by a steel plate fitted on the back of the contact point, not by direct welding or pop riveting. After any accident or heavy crash, harnesses must be inspected and replaced.  The mounting points musts be inspected and re-checked. Belts showing wear or cuts should be replaced immediately.
