For street, road, track or competition use, the choice of seat is an important one. Sparco's years of experience in World Rally Championships, European Touring Car Championships, Mickey Thompson Off Road Series, IMSA Endurance Racing, SCCA Pro and Club Racing have provided a testing ground under the most extreme conditions. Experience learned in these arenas allow Sparco to bring you the finest, toughest, strongest competition and road seats available anywhere in the world.

 

EVO

Sparco "Evo" seat was designed with an emphasis on comfort in a high-tension environment, the shell features sandwich-style layers (carbon, Kevlar, carbon) for extra strength and endurance without compromising weight - still less than 9.7 lbs. net. This seat is also available in a fiberglass shell version.
