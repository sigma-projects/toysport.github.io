Welcome to Toysport Internet Drive.  This is the next best thing to an actual visit.

For many of our customers, a visit to Toysport is a surprise part of a vacation. Families from around the world come to visit the many sites of Southern California, and frequently the Toyota diehard in the family- manages to squeeze in time to visit our showroom and facility (the whole family in tow). Out-of-town business people often schedule LA meetings with added free time to drop-in.  Toyota people from the different Regional offices, in town for Toyota corporate headquarters business, drop-in regularly to see what’s new. Race weekends bring car clubs and motor sports enthusiasts to continue bench-racing.  Long holiday weekends bring drive-ins from all over California. Often, Toysport is the first and last stop, of an LA visit- first to order parts and then to pick-up the goods on the way home.

We have not disappointed our visitors- judging by their excited reaction when they see the most comprehensive display of Toyota sports parts. Some Toyota items that only we have made the visit totally worthwhile. Often, new inquiries and old questions are answered by a simple visit. Like a “tourist trap”- Toyota curios, gadgets, engines, accessories, and hardware are here to view, hold, examine, compare, and (hopefully!) purchase.  Sometimes it is to meet the face of the voice on the phone or behind the e-mail address

There is nothing like Toysport, if you are a Toyota enthusiast. We are true Toyotaphiles. We enjoy and pamper our Toyotas. We are dedicated to making your Toyota, into the best Toyota. Like you, we are Toyota obsessed.

Nothing that will replace a "brick and mortar" business. In this age of Ebay, did you ever wonder who actually stocks and more important- knows the products? Know-how and experience is developed by installing, using, testing, and evaluating the products. Hands on experience.  Naturally, some technical information we have will need to remain proprietary. Some chat room denizens or websites in "cyberspace" claim a lot of experience, be careful and qualify what you read.  Some sites have a lot of information but also more- misinformation!
