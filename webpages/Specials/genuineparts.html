The different Toyota National Parts Distribution system is tightly inter-linked and monitored by Toyota Motor Corp Japan.  Genuine replacement parts ordered through the different National Distributors are centrally monitored.  Although the majority of parts originate from Japan- some parts cannot be directly ordered from there.  Specific parts can only be ordered from specific National Distributors.  Some parts identified as non-import parts in their respective domestic markets need substantial additional paperwork to order- if they can be ordered at all.
