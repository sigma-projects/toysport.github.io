PURCHASE PROGRAMS

TOYOTA / LEXUS / SCION DEALERS:

We will accept orders covered by a Purchase Order signed by the Dealer’s Part Manager only. We will need your Dealer Code. COD Company Check will be accepted. Dealer employees may order directly from Toysport, but orders must be pre-paid. California Toyota Dealers must submit copies of the CA Seller’s Permit and a signed Resale Card.

GROUP PURCHASE:

Special Group Purchase deals are available. We will accept SGP’s as a 1-time order, 1 person will be responsible for SGP. Drop shipment is OK, and shipping will be charged separately.  Minimum quantity requirements must be met, before the order is accepted.

TOYSPORT DEALERS:

All current dealers must have on file all contact information, copies of business license, reseller’s permit, or export permit. If you have not purchased in the last previous year, you have to resend your bank and/or credit card information. Your orders should be faxed; this will be used as order confirmation also.
