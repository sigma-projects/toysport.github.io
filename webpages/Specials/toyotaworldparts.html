Toysport has been importing Genuine Toyota Parts and Accessories from the different international markets through the different Toyota National Distributors.  This has been the most prolific source of “original quality” TOYOTA parts for our customers- but also the most frustrating and inaccessible source.  Although the majority of parts come from Japan- you cannot order all the parts directly from there.  Specific parts can only be ordered from specific National Distributors.

The Toyota World-Wide Parts distribution is tightly inter-linked and monitored .  Genuine replacement parts ordered through all National Distributors are centrally monitored. Some parts identified as non-import parts for their respective domestic markets need substantial additional paperwork to order- if they can be ordered at all.

TOYOTA parts for the different markets are priced differently.  Parts with the same exact Part #s maybe priced as much 50% more between other foreign markets.  The best source for pricing and warranty is still your local TOYOTA dealer.   In the U.S. however, Toysport can supply competitively priced Toyota, Lexus, and Scion parts and accessories.

Market destination of the different Toyota vehicles dictates what is available for the particular models in the marketing region.  Naturally there are different variations on even the most common replacement parts.

Toysport imports and sells these TOYOTA Genuine Parts and Accessories for our world-wide customers. Toyota parts imported by Toysport carry NO PART numbers.  All TOYOTA parts imported by Toysport are labeled and identified as such.  These TOYOTA parts carry NO WARRANTY and are sold as SPORTS PARTS.  Please read our disclaimer.

This is to prevent end-users for applying for warranty; and avoid liability issues for the respective TOYOTA National Distributors in the various countries.
