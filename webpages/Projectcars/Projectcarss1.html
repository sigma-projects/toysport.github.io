PROJECT CARS                                                               THE LIST

These are the Toysport Project Cars.  This is our resume.  We listed all the different chassis and engine combinations we had actually worked on (and can remember…..).

Some in the list we had done several of, with minor detail differences.  Some built out of challenge, some out of boredom.  Some built on inspiration, some with obsession.  Some to simply win a bet it could be done!!  Some cobbled together on the leanest budget, some on deep pocket unlimited funding.  Some built and finished on schedule, most took forever to finish …..

We stopped counting at 300+ and trimmed the list to similar combinations.  (Our apologies if your car was left out, call us so we can add it on.)  The list still looks mighty impressive !

From the most exclusive Toyota models to the most important Toyota Corporate VIP – Toysport has worked on all of them for all of them. There were as many reasons to build these cars or engines, as there are chassis/engine combinations Toyota has produced through the years.  Some conversions, we refused outright when it did not make sense.  

Some were featured magazine cars (from the most reputable magazines to the editorially challenged life-style types); some were built for display or car shows (from Toyota commercials / public relation events to the local High Schools auto fairs); some were entered and prepared for competition- winners in ALL.  Internationally several were featured in Japanese video / magazines and most recently by a Canadian TV show.

The most common comments – “Toyota should have done it this way!”; “You sure it will work?”; “It will take HOW LONG?”; and of course the ever hopeful “You do sponsorships?”  Most started by a simple “Can you ….. ?”

Been there, done that.  When Toysport talks Toyota, you know right away: Toysport knows Toyota cars, engines, conversions, and parts.  (Maybe not in the minutest and most obsessive compulsive detail, but unparalleled in hands on experience.)

Talent and time.  Money and manuals. 

Enjoy reading the articles.
