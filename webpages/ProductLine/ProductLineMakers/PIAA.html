The Global leader in halogen lamp systems, PIAA Corporation was established in 1963 with the commitment to manufacture world-class products that our customers could use with pride and confidence. Today PIAA upholds that commitment by combining market driven concepts with the latest technology to make night and inclement weather driving as safe as possible.

Technology
Who's the proven leader in auxiliary lighting? PIAA. Discerning drivers indicate that PIAA's Ion Crystal™ technology far surpasses the fog light performance of traditional amber lenses. Likewise, PIAA's Platinum Series "Super White" technology provides a white light approaching the brightness and intensity of High Intensity Discharge (H.I.D.) lamps at a fraction of the cost.

Race Proven Quality
Dependable and reliable under the most severe racing conditions," note racing professionals. PIAA quality makes PIAA lighting systems the serious driver's choice. PIAA lenses are computer designed for optimum performance and are made of hardened prism cut glass to withstand the rigors of off road racing and normal highway hazards. PIAA's lamps feature a specially designed polyphenylene sulfide composite, aluminum or stamped steel housing. These materials make it possible for the lamps to operate using higher wattage bulbs without sacrificing bulb life.

High Intensity Discharge
PIAA is proud to introduce a new generation of High Intensity Discharge (H.I.D.) driving lamps. If your looking for the most advanced lighting technology on the market today then look no further than PIAA's new H.I.D. system. The first thing you'll notice about PIAA's new H.I.D. system is its compact size and contoured lens. But don't let the compact size fool you. PIAA's new H.I.D. driving light with its superior performance, compact size, and affordability is the ultimate choice of serious automotive enthusiasts.
