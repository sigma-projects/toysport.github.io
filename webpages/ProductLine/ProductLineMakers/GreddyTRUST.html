TOYOTA APPLICATION GRACIER
The GRACER Airinx represents the first new "science" in air filters in years. To provide maximum air flow and filtration,
we start with a bigger filter. For vehicles that use flat-panel filters, we have Airinx II. The two-playbonded foam provides
 superior flow and filtration before and after cleaning.  The Airinx has a high-capacity wire mesh outer shell and an inner funnel
of high-quality, lightweight ABS rosin. The primary filtering medium is a washable urethane.  No "pre-oiling" is necessary. 
Two densities protect the turbocharger and engine from damaging dirt and debris. Our dyno testing has shown consistent
horsepower increases.
