For over 25 Years ARIAS has led the industry in setting the standard for custom made forged pistons. An extensive line of forging blanks had enabled ARIAS to manufacture virtually any piston in the motor sports world. Whether you are building an engine for drag racing, oval track, road race, off road, historic/antique restoration, marine, 4-stroke motorcycles, airplanes or simply street use, you may rely on ARIAS to help you meet your needs. ARIAS can supply your pistons in either 2618 heavy duty or 4032 high silicon forged aluminum.
