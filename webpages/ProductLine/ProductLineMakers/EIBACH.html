In the middle of Europe - in the heart of Germany - lies the delightful central mountain region called "Sauerland". 

The Sauerland, which is a part of Westphalia and is also referred to as the land of the thousand mountains.  It has been known for centuries for its production of iron and for steel processing. Virtues and abilities relating to crafts are values that continue to be held in high esteem today and are the pride and tradition of many Sauerland families. 

This was the cradle of Eibach. This is where Heinrich Eibach started his production of single springs on a purely handcrafted basis in a tiny hut almost 50 years ago. He was a learned spring maker and already had decades of experience. His passion for the product, for innovative production technology, as well as his commitment to highest quality workmanship is the elixir of the Eibach Group today.

Until the 70's, Eibach produced springs for industrial applications exclusively. It was not until the formation of the German Tuning-Industry that Eibach became the sought-after partner for chassis springs production.

Today, Eibach is the worldwide leader of high quality chassis technology. They are also involved in Industrial Springs, Dynaflex Die-Springs and Seal Components, High Performance Valve Springs, Children's Playground Components and more.

In addition to the ultra modern parent plant in Finnentrop, Germany, there is a similar facility in Irvine, California.  Eibach first moved beyond the Sauerland with the development and production of high-quality racing shocks in Great Britain.  Now Eibach is represented by Engineering and Marketing firms in Japan and Australia and is also represented by exclusive importers in about 50 other countries.

Today, as in the past, Eibach is a family owned and operated company; now in the second and third generation. Its style remains unchanged – Eibach is committed to products with its heart and its intellect. Eibach’s strength is in providing market and customer-specific solutions by collaborating with our partners through simultaneous Engineering.

The Eibach Philosophy

For almost 5 decades, the entire Eibach Team, including all employees, has worked within a special culture in our relations with customers, vendors and other partners leading Eibach to today's global position. This internal culture comes from the heart of all our employees and is not artificially created.

Two years ago, Eibach began to associate the words Tension & Balance - as physical elements - to their company Philosophy. They transformed this perceived identity into a visual image as it relates to all aspects of their business.

Eibach’s philosophy starts with the relationship between Owners, Management and the Employees. This relationship is the main element of their entire company culture. Eibach feels that these relationships are balanced by unconditional mutual respect, fairness, honesty and loyalty - it is quite simply, the mutual care for each other’s well being. These mutual relationships can't be split into classes or groups. This means all Eibach’s business partners - on the premises of their loyalty - can expect from Eibach a behavior influenced by this Balance.

This is the one element of Eibach’s culture we are most proud of and work diligently to maintain.

However, this element alone is not able to move mountains – we, as well as Eibach, need the kick, the excitement and most of all, the passion. This means constantly setting goals and monitoring tasks in order to reach these goals. This is where Tension comes into play. Tension is symbolized by the excitement, drive, and passion to develop new products or open new markets, to obtain even higher quality levels, to enjoy the mutual success in motorsports, etc., etc., etc.

This Tension can only be balanced with TeamWork and Team Spirit, which grows stronger with new challenges.  Only within such a positive sphere of Balanced Tension, can one realize the joy of personal performance which in turn positively effects the mental attitude releasing personal power to excel in one's work.

This is Eibach’s company philosophy, which drives their passion to be one of the premier spring producers worldwide as well as being known as Eibach...The Suspension Company.

Eibach in Motorsports...Your Turn to Win.

Since many years, Eibach Springs has ambitiously sought after and successfully met the high demands of Motorsports worldwide. Eibach’s dedication to R&D, engineering and manufacturing has pioneered and set new standards for quality control, consistent performance and durability of racing suspension systems.

This uncompromising technology and performance is available for both open wheel and full bodied race cars throughout the world...from the extreme limits of Formula 1 to Formula Fords from on-the-edge Sprint Cars to thundering NASCAR... Championship racing teams from around the globe rely on Eibach.

Leading teams such as Alfa, Audi, BMW, Citroën, Dallara, Ferrari, Fiat, Ford Motorsport, Honda, Holden, McLaren, Mazda, Mitsubishi Ralliart, Nissan, TTE Toyota, TRD, Prodrive Subaru, Sauber, Schnitzer, Benetton, Williams, Jordan, Tyrell, Peugeot Sport, Abt, Rosberg, Opel, SMS,  Holzer and many more have trusted in Eibach.

Eibach has been successfully involved in every kind of Motorsports throughout the World.

Formula vehicles - Formula 1, Champ Cars, Formula 3000, F3, IRL Sports Cars - Prototypes USA - CanAm, ISRS, GT-Cars Rallye - Rallye World Championship as well as other Rallye national championships Touring Cars - Super Touring Cars in Germany, Italy, France, England, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, South America Ford Escort Cup, Puma Cup and Saxo Cup USA Oval Track - Winston Cup NASCAR, Bush Grand National USA Short Track - Dirt and Asphalt, Sprints and Midgets Off-Road / Rallye Raid - Dakar, Baja 1000, SCORE Int'l Off Road

The world famous Eibach Race Spring (ERS) System consists of over 700 main and tender "coil-over" springs allowing the basic to the most complex suspension systems to be fine-tuned to any track condition. Whether your suspension requires a linear rate (single-spring), or a progressive characteristic (dual/multiple-spring), our ERS system has the winning combination.

Eibach Super Speedway (ESS) Springs offer a complete range of precision engineered conventional springs designed to meet the demanding weight and track conditions associated with road-course, short-track oval and Super Speedway racing.
