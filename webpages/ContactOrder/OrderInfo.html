HOW TO ORDER

Ordering from the Toysport is easy and convenient. But first- Please read our Terms and Conditions, Website Policy, and Sports Parts Notice.

To order ONLINE:  You can use the Online Shop.  Orders are processed electronically and efficiently in a safe secure method.  There are pictures and product descriptions to help in your selection. There is an exclusive e-mail link to ask questions if you need additional help.  Only PayPal is currently as payment method.

To order by phone: (323.777.7676) If you have technical questions or need to talk LIVE, a Toysport parts specialist can assist you.  Please have all your Credit Card information ready.  We accept MasterCard, Visa, PayPal, and Discover cards. Credit card orders can only be shipped to the billing address and is verified.  Items cannot be delivered to a PO Box.  No COD or purchase orders are accepted.

To order by fax: (323.755.1176) available 24 hours a day. For International orders this is the preferred method.  Please use our ORDER FORM. Print and fill in all the required information. Provide us with a contact or fax number where we can reach you if there are any questions with the order.

To order by mail: Please use our ORDER FORM. Or provide the part number and/or description of the items being ordered. Print and fill in all the required information.  Provide us with an e-mail address or phone number where we can reach you if there are any questions with the order. Mail to: TOYSPORT 1547 W El Segundo Blvd. Gardena CA 90249

PAYMENT METHOD:  We require prepayment for all orders.  For other than accepted credit cards, please send Western Union Money Order or Cashiers check.  Wire transfer instructions for International Orders will be forwarded when requested.

Special Orders: We accept Special Orders for parts not normally carried in stock. A 50% deposit or full payment may be required. Deposits are non-refundable and orders non-cancelable. We cannot accept returns on SPECIAL ORDER parts. Inventory of certain items listed in this Toysport site is limited and may not be available for immediate delivery. Please read our Terms and Conditions.

Shipping and Handling Charges: Shipping and handling charges, and applicable sales tax (California shipments only are subject to sales tax), are added to your invoice. Items that are too large or heavy will be shipped by truck. Delivery charges vary according to the weight of your order. For details and cost of handling and delivery charges, ask your Toysport representative. Please read our Shipping Policy.

PRODUCT  SPECIFICATIONS and PRICING

Product specifications, availability, and pricing are all subject to change without notice. Special Sale items are limited quantity and events are for a limited period. In store pricing may be different.
