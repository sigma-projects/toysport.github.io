Toysport has listed various engine components manufactured for us by the leading suppliers of Pistons, Rods, Valves, Turbos, etc.…. These sports parts incorporate unique and proprietary product specifications that Toysport has designed into these components. We have identified some manufacturers to show the source of the products. Toysport products will be different from what the manufacturers would normally sell directly to the public or other vendors. In most cases the products will not work, as designed, with other components from other sources or even from the same manufacturer. We custom order our parts to support inventory levels and in quantity to avail of volume pricing.

When we sell engine component parts, we always ask the customers, what other parts they are using. Naturally choosing and matching components is the most important first step in any engine or project build-up. The success and failure of your project will depend on proper planning.
