These carburetor kits come complete, including all necessary hardware; with TOYSPORT’s Toyota experience the WEBER KITS are available for a wide range of applications precisely-jetted for their specific model (stock engine) and for performance applications (modified engines), there is a correct jetting upgrade.                                                                                               

                                      

                                                32/36 DGVE Series Carburetor 

The basic upgrade is the 32/36 DGVE  This is a perfect performance replacement carburetor that increases performance, improves drivability, and fuel economy- up to 2400cc .  Modified engines to 1800cc are a perfect match.

The 38 DGAS is the choice for engines over 2000cc with modifications.  The 38 has a larger fuel delivery system and can handle a broader range of engine modifications. This synchronous carburetor will get less fuel economy.

Both carburetors are available with water or electric choke.  The DGVE is also available with a manual choke. Please see the TOYSPORT WEBER application chart.  Applications for other than Toyota are also available, please call.
