Non-squeal, fade resistant, low dust, the Meta Master Brake Pads are a semi-metallic, non-asbestos rigid molded material that will withstand high pressures and has wear resistance that will not damage rotors. The friction level remains stable through a wide range of operating temperatures, pressures and rubbing speeds.
