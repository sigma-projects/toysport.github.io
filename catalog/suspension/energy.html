Energy Suspension

Energy Suspension is without a doubt the world leader in polyurethane suspension components. Our innovative designs have set the standards for the industry. energy Suspension polyurethane bushings give crisp response on the street and add precision launches at the strip. Unwanted movement of suspension components can cause breakage and vague handling characteristics. Big, soft rubber bushings allow the sway bars, shocks, control arms etc. to deflect and move all over. Energy Suspension bushings are full floating and non-binding, allowing proper weight transfer and full suspension travel. Application ranges from Strut Rod Bushings to Bump Stops to Sway Bar Bushings, comes in a variety of colors.
