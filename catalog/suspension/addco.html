ADDCO Sway Bars

ADDCO Industries began designing anti-sway bar handling kits in 1962 when aftermarket suspension improvement was in its infancy. As interest in handling grew among auto enthusiasts during the next three decades, our original four applications grew to over six hundred. Today, as the world's largest aftermarket manufacturer of anti-sway bar handling kits, ADDCO produces kits for almost all domestic and import, cars, trucks and vans.
