Tokico

Tokico has over 50 years experience in the manufacturing of hydraulic suspension components, a long time supplier of OEM shocks for most major auto manufacturers, domestic and foreign. Tokico was the first company to develop the new twin tube low pressure gas technology that is so popular nowadays. Tokico is well equipped to respond to the demand for quality shock absorbers which offer both proper vehicle control and good ride quality. Suspension products by Tokico include standard shock absorber, manually and electronically adjustable shock absorbers, and high performance springs.

TOKICO HP HIGH PERFORMANCE SHOCK ABSORBER
Tokico's premimum High Performance shock absorbers use a twin tube low pressure gas design. Combine this with the multi-stage variable aperture sandwich valve system achieves a shock with excellent damping characteristics for vehicle control and stability. At the same time Tokico tailors the damping curves to reduce the harshness usually found in other performance shock absorbers.

Tokico's externally adjustable Illumina series offers the highest shock absorber technology currently available in the aftermarket. The ease of adjustment and change in vehicle response is second to none. The Illumina uses a specially calibrated orifice bypass system which allows simultaneous adjustment of both compresion and rebound. Each adjustment "clicks" so you always know your shock setting. The adjustment range of Illumina is one of the widest available, with 5 separate positions. Position 1 is the softest, or lowest damping force, while position 5 is the stiffest.

TOKICO HIGH PERFORMANCE SPRING SET
There are two major reasons for changing thes prings on a vehicle being driven on the street. The most common is to decrease ride height for cosmetic purposes, the other is to improve handling. The combination of lower height ride and stiffer springs generally improves handling in a noticeable fashion. Tokico Performance Springs are mde from the finest alloy springs metal. Depending upon the application the spring may be progressive or linear and they are designed to lower the vehicle between 3/4" to 1.25". Tokico Performance Springs have an increase in spring rate between 15% and 40% over stock springs.

For a limited number of applications, Tokico manufactures electronically adjustable shock absorbers which is called Illumina II. These shock absorbers are adjustable from the drivers position if the driver desires, and while the vehicle is in motion. Illumina II electronic shock system are the ultimate in aftermarket shock absorber technology. You can get sporty handling or boulevard rise with a twist of a switch.
