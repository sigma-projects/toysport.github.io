SHOCKS
KYB / KONI / TRD

For the budget conscious, Toysport recommends KYB. KYB features monotube designs and higher gas pressure to keep your car's tires glued to the road surface. They provide unsurpassed control on even the most demanding roads. KYB Gas-a-just both radial and bias ply tires. The GR-2 features lower pressure for riding comfort, along with the improved handling and response only gas shocks can provide, even in small cars. It is also an economical way to step up from the hydraulic shocks to the quality ride and control of gas shocks.
