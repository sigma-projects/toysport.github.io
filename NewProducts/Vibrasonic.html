TOYSPORT PET VIBRASONIC Seat by Toyota

Another first.  Experience the ultimate comfort of a Vibrasonic equipped seats.  Discover the unique benefits of the latest Toyota Innovation on their ultimate top of the line models.

The Toyota Century and the soon to be introduced 2001 Lexus LS 430 come with unique Vibrasonic equipped seats.  Developed by Toyota Group engineers (with input from medical professionals- this unit will install in minutes without seat modifications on all Toyota and Lexus models.  It becomes an integral and functional part of a total interior system.

The Vibrasonic unit is manufactured and supplied to Toyota by Aisin Seiki, a member of Toyota’s exclusive group of OEM suppliers.  Toysport PET imported this accessory for Toyota and Lexus owners the opportunity to upgrade their seats and enjoy the latest seat option from Toyota.
