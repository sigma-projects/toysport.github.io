WEBER DCOE SERIES CARBURETORS

Still the standard by which all other carburetors are measured, the DCOE is the ultimate side draft carburetor. It is unsurpassed for full-race use, yet interchangeable calibrated parts allow it to be tailored to suit even the mildest engine and application situation. TOYSPORT DCOE kits include all necessary hardware. 

                                                                                                     

 

The kits are available with 40mm or 45mm carburetors.  Minor rejetting or recalibration will be required for highly modified engines.   Air filters are available, but due to different configurations- must be ordered separately.

Please see the TOYSPORT WEBER application chart.  Applications for other than Toyota are also available, please call.                    

TOYSPORT has been a  WEBER distributor for many years and has one of the largest inventories  of both small parts and complete carburetors. 

TOYSPORT stocks the complete range of WEBER performance carburetors as well as all currently available parts. Due to the long life of the WEBER carburetor and its many years in production some parts as well as specific OEM carburetors types may no longer be in production.
