SHIPPING POLICY

UPS Ground Service in the continental U.S. is used (unless otherwise requested). Orders received by 11:00 am PST for in-stock parts are shipped on the same day. The UPS weight limit is 150 pounds per package. Overnight and express shipping can be arranged for an additional charge.

UPS 2 Day Air Service delivers in-stock parts within two working days

UPS 3 Day Select delivers in-stock parts within 3 working days.

UPS Next Day Air guarantees in-stock parts ordered by 12:00 pm PST will arrive by the next day in most areas of the continental U.S.

UPS Saturday Delivery guarantees in-stock parts ordered between 12:00 pm PST Thursday and 12:00 pm PST Friday will be delivered by 3:30 pm Saturday. There is an additional $10.00 per package charge for this service over UPS Next Day Air shipping fees! UPS makes one attempt to deliver on Saturday; some areas are not covered--call us for details.

US Postal Service will be charged an extra $5.00 per shipment, over USPS charges.

Overseas orders will be charged $20.00 per order handling charge, to cover export documentation and packing. Overseas, as well as APO and FPO, orders and shipping charges must be paid in advance. Forwarding, Clearance, and Customs duties, if applicable, are your responsibility.

Oversize and Overweight Parts too large or too heavy for UPS are delivered by truck. Call us for an estimate. All shipments need to be pre-paid, and will be adjusted after the pick-up is made.

Engine or Body Kits will have extra handling charge. Engines on a pallet or a boxed will be charged $20 - $50. Body kits will be charged $10 per box. This includes APO and FPO orders.

Damaged Shipment  Inspect your packages! If any package arrives and shows signs of external damage or tampering, DO NOT ACCEPT IT! Refuse it, and ask your carrier to advise of their procedure to follow. Contact our Customer Service department immediately. If there is concealed damage, please notify us and we will advise you of the procedure to follow.
