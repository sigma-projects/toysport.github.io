Toysport can supply more Genuine Parts than Toyota USA.

Toysport supports Toyota enthusiasts / owners worldwide with original Toyota parts from and for all the international Toyota markets.  There are many different Toyota models sold in various versions throughout the world- North American, European, Asian, World Market, Oceanic, Japanese, and some other sub-categories.  There is a wealth of unique market changes that allow you to make your vehicle, subtlety different or totally foreign.  Toysport is your direct link to all these parts.  

 For Toysport to order the correct application for these parts- we need the VIN # (Vehicle Identification Number) or Chassis # and Engine Type and Serial # of your vehicle and/or the target model.  For alternate or conversion parts we still need these #s, so we can properly identify the foreign market model and verify the possible application and interchange.

 Due to the various smog and safety specifications in the different world markets, Toysport cannot assume any liability in fitness and legality of these imported parts.  These imported parts will be accepted for order and sold as  SPORTS PARTS  -that may not be used on US Public Highways.  Also to prevent any issues or problems with any Toyota organization- we do not identify these imported parts with any Toyota Part #s.  There is absolutely no warranty on these parts, from Toysport or Toyota.  We reserve the right to refuse any order.

Parts special ordered from the different international Toyota Parts Distribution centers, normally take 6-8 weeks to receive (if the unit is in stock at the foreign PDC); sometimes sooner, sometimes longer.  It all depends on availability of the part, the scheduled shipping date, special handling required, and the method the order is shipped from the different international PDC warehouses.  Ultimately, it will depend if TMC (Japan) has the part.

The process of ordering is painstaking and complicated.  It can become very expensive to process and fill an order, specially for parts for older (7 year and above) vehicle models.  Our extensive experience and resources allow us to offer this service- but do not expect to save money if the part is available at your national Toyota warehouse or your local Toyota dealer.
