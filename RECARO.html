RECARO is the world’s leading manufacturer of specialty automotive seats, setting the industry standards for quality, craftsmanship, style, comfort, and ergonomics in individualized seating.  RECARO is the premier supplier of ergonomic seats for the individualist and has the highest brand recognition in aftermarket seating in the world. RECARO seats fit the widest range of vehicle applications and are backed by an unparalleled, 3-year factory warranty.

RECARO has a simple design philosophy that states a seat should adjust to fit the individual. RECARO seats are enhanced by innovative technologies that allow drivers to customize and individualize their seats: Airmatic®, Vent® and Artline. The phrase "Made by RECARO" on the seat means something special.

Racing

RECARO made its name in motorsport racing as the seat of choice for many winning drivers. Since the 1960s, RECARO has been the preferred seat for the world's great racing series... LeMans, Sebring Daytona, and Mille Miglia have been won in RECARO seats.

RECARO's unique sandwich structure, glass fiber reinforced plastic (GRP) with ultra-lightweight Kevlar, provide maximal strength and minimal weight. RECARO's racing shells meet appropriate FIA regulations. For the racing market, RECARO offers:

Pole Position

A true motorsports racing shell virtually tailored to the driver's body. Its anatomically shaped frame, wide seat cushion area and excellent lateral support allow for maximal driver comfort under extreme stress.

 

Standard Features:

  • Highly stable, glass fiber reinforced plastic (GRP)

  • Wide seat cushion area
