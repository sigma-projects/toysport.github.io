Time Tested & Race Proven

With over 30 years of experience and nearly 1,700 products, Moroso is your one-stop performance source for Drag and Oval Track Racing, Street Machines, and Show Cars. Our race-proven products are designed and manufactured with a formidable combination of computer-controlled equipment, quality materials, and expert craftsmanship.

As a leader in innovation, Moroso has developed many products that have become commonplace in racing today such as deep sump oil pans, tall valve covers, electric water pumps, solid motor mounts, and many more. This innovative tradition continues as Moroso engineers continually develop new products through our close working relationship with the nation's top engine builders and race teams. Extensive research and development, on-track testing, and a steadfast commitment to quality are the cornerstones on which Moroso has earned its reputation for over three decades.

Moroso Performance Products is one of the worlds most respected suppliers of automotive equipment for racing and street performance applications. Moroso manufacturers nearly 1700 products, from oil pans and oiling system accessories, to valve covers, ignition components, fuel system equipment, air cleaners, hard core racing tools and numerous other performance parts. The company has an impressive list of customers, ranging from grass-roots racers to top level NASCAR Winston Cup and NHRA/IHRA race teams. In addition to the Connecticut based manufacturing operation, the company also owns Moroso Motorsports Park located in West Palm Beach, Florida. This is a multi-use facility including a 2.25 mile, ten turn road course and a state-of-the-art NHRA sanctioned drag strip.

Moroso was started in 1968 by Dick Moroso, one of the most successful modified production drag racers on record. Dick left his racing career to design, test and manufacture specialized products aimed exclusively at racer's needs. Racers quickly recognized that Moroso products were a cut above the others because they were designed and built for racers - by racers!

Dick Moroso was responsible for many innovations in the high performance industry. He was instrumental in nurturing the warehouse distribution (WD) network, which became an integral step in supplying the growing number of speed shops appearing in every corner of the country. In addition, Moroso pioneered several racing products including deep-sump oil pans, electric water pumps, tall valve covers, lightweight front drag tires, racing vacuum pumps, and others.

In November 1998, while the company was celebrating its 30th anniversary, Dick Moroso lost a year-long battle with cancer.

The Moroso tradition of innovation, quality and integrity is carried forward by his son Rick, who in 1997 was named president of Moroso Performance Products. Rick also carries on the Moroso drag racing legacy, campaigning the seriously updated 1961 Corvette that his dad raced during the 60's.

The Moroso heritage also continues in manufacturing and quality control. Computer systems with the latest CAD/CAM software have replaced the drafting tables and the Moroso plant is now filled with sophisticated CNC machinery including turret punch presses, turning centers, lathes and robotic welders. The combination of highly skilled craftsmen and highly accurate machinery guarantees that the tradition of quality started by Dick Moroso over 30 years ago will continue throughout the coming decades.

As Morosp looks forward to the future you can count on Moroso's continued commitment to quality, technical support and racing. We have renewed our long-standing contingency relationship with NASCAR, NHRA and IHRA, and have made new commitments to other sanctioning bodies such as the Contingency Connection, IMCA, NMCA and NIRA.
