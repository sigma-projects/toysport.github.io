VDO

VDO North America is a leading supplier of electronic, information, and control systems for original equipment manufacturers in the automotive, heavy duty, industrial, marine, and power sports industries, as well as instrumentation and electrical products to the automotive, marine, and industrial aftermarkets. An acknowledged world leader in instrumentation, VDO also produces a
wide range of vehicle systems and components including complete cockpit and dashboard modules, complete truck cabs, engine and fuel management modules, cruise controls, throttle body assemblies, and sensors. VDO is an ISO 9001 / QS9000 certified manufacturer.

VDO North America’s parent company, Mannesmann AG, is a global technology company based in Germany. One of the world’s top 100 industrial companies, Mannesmann has operations worldwide and employs over 120,000 people. The company is active in four major sectors: Industrial Technology (machinery and engineering); Automotive Engineering (electronic information and control systems, driveline and chassis components and systems); Telecommunications (mobile telephone systems, telecommunication services, and information technology); and Tubes and Trading (tubular products, trading and logistics).
