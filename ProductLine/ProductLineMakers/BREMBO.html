BREMBO

Evolution- a new breed of performance.

Evolution is defined as "A gradual process in which something changes into a different and usually better or more complex form." This concept perfectly describes the development of Brembo's High-Performance Brake Kits. The knowledge gained through making the world's best original equipment and competition brake system components has been combined to create the best available high-performance braking systems.

Brembo High-Performance Brake Kits include 12- to 14-inch cross-drilled cadmium-plated rotors. These are the same rotors that are used on numerous championship-winning racecars. Billet aluminum hats are vehicle specific and have been machined to exacting specifications. The hats and rotors combine to create a "floating" disc which improves brake performance, pedal feel and heat related stress

Brembo's aluminum four-piston calipers utilize sequentially sized pistons to reduce tapered wear of the included high-performance brake pads. These calipers are mounted with specially machined billet brackets. The vehicle's hydraulic system is connected to the calipers with Goodridge brand DOT and TÜV approved stainless steel braided brake lines.

The extensive brake system knowledge gained by Brembo engineers over the past 38 years allows us to produce big brake kits that provide superior braking performance while maintaining the reliability required in a street vehicle. And, while their design focuses o function rather than form, they look great, especially with today's plus-sized open-design wheels.

The process of developing Brembo's High Performance Brake Kits has been a gradual one, and the result is definitely a better and more complex form of brake system.
