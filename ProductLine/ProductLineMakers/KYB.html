KYB (KAYABA)

KYB has developed a wide variety of quality Ride Control Products to improve the efficiency, stability and safety of numerous vehicles and other products.

KYB offer a complete line of ride control products for both domestic and import vehicles, passenger cars, sports cars, trucks and sport utility vehicles. Whether you're looking for OEM type replacement struts and shocks or a performance upgrade for your vehicle, KYB has the correct product for you in either our GR-2 lines or our Gas-a-Just monotube shock absorbers.

The high quality of KYB ride control products is unsurpassed by any other world manufacturer. KYB products are world known for their superb fit and finish as well as their consistent performance under all types of driving conditions.
