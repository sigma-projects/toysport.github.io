AUTOPOWER

Autopower Industries is the oldest manufacturer of quality sports car/racing equipment and the first roll bar manufacturer to be licensed and certified by the Sports Car Club of America. High standards of workmanship go into every Autopower product. All roll bars and cages are precision machined and MIG welded. Roll cage kits can be added to the majority of the vehicles, these are designed to fit each individual car model and is not a universal kit. All bolts, nuts and back-up plates are included. Most tubing size is 1 3/4" x .120 unless noted otherwise. The sedan roll bars is distinctively styled to match the contour of the car for which it is designed for. All feature 4 point mounting for safety and chassis stiffening. The racing bar has a diagonal cross bar plus a head restraint. An optional roll bar cover is also available, it is designed to enhance the appearance of the roll bar and to provide safety in padding the roll bar against casual contact.
