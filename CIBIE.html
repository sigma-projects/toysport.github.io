Beam Type code:

AB - Fog Beam:  Range from 50 to 70 meters.  Clear Horizontal cut to avoid self-dazzling.  Very wide lighting providing a good view of the verge and in the bends.  It must be placed as low as possible to obtain maximum effectiveness.

 

LP - Drive Beam:  Range from 250 to 400 meters.  Long range and cornering beam.  Long visibility distance in front and on the sides.

 

LPR - Radar - Drive type Beam:  Range of 370 meters.  Anti-dazzle system of the headlamp eliminates rising rays.  It allows a good view of the road even in very foggy weather.  In clear weather, it resembles drive beam.

 

VA - Cornering Beam:  Range of 140 meters.  Intermediate lighting, between fog and drive beams.  Very homogeneous lighting on a range of more than 40°.  A very suitable beam for sport driving on winding roads.

 

SP - Spot-Long Range Beam:  Range from 500 to 600 meters.  Long, deep narrow beam.  Maximum luminosity concentrated in front of the car.
