Stage 1 & 2 Body Kit Packages are all painted in factory colors and utilize existing attachment points.  They include an extended rear valence panel and rocker panel skirts. The packages vary by front end treatment.  

 

Sportivo Suspension Set includes high-performance shock absorbers, lowering springs (approx. .75 inch), rear upper strut mount supports, front and rear sway-bars with stiffer bushings, front stabilizer bushings and rear suspension member spacers.  This set has been tuned to provide a more responsive, firm handling vehicle but maintain a comfortable ride.

 

TRD Front & Rear Strut Tower Braces are designed to minimize body flex during aggressive cornering.

 

TRD High-Performance Air Filter provides a lower restriction path for incoming air.
