TOYSPORT PET PRODUCTS

To distinguish exclusive Toysport parts from similar Toyota parts available from some of our suppliers, we are introducing our proprietary Toysport products under the PET designation.  Toysport PET (Performance Engineered Toyota) will identify all unique Toysport parts.  Although we have been selling some products under our suppliers’ brand name, most of our parts are uniquely our own design and specification.  We feel this new designation will proudly differentiate and identify our products, as Toysport’s own.

Under PET, we will also identify parts we have directly imported, and parts not available from official / established U.S. importers.  This is being done, again, to distinguish and identify parts exclusively imported by Toysport.  Our PET direct imports are fully supported by Toysport for warranty, replacement parts, technical information, and other customer support issues.  Naturally some official U.S. importer will not be able to offer customer support for this exclusive imports.

All PET parts will be identified with Toysport markings.  This mark will identify original products and direct imports.  This mark will assure the customers that they can call Toysport for technical support, and prevent other vendors and third parties from claiming products as Toysport issue.  We have always been concerned about this illegal practice- of people claiming and using Toysport as the source of parts and engines that we never sold, modified, or produced.

TRD SPORTS PARTS

Toysport has been distributing TRD parts in the U.S. since 1980 and overseas since 1974.  With direct access to TRD (Technocraft) Japan and TRD USA in California- we have available the fastest and most accurate delivery for any TRD product in the world- and to any worldwide location.  There are two distinct TRD lines- TRD Japan and TRD USA.  TRD Japan is the ORIGINAL source for all TRD products, and TRD USA has US products designed exclusively for the US market.  We ship directly from Japan, Manila, and the U.S.  We specialize in world-wide import and export of all TRD sports parts, and are recognized experts in product support for these parts.  For mainland USA customers, the local TOYOTA Dealer is the most convenient place to purchase TRD USA products.  We welcome and service all International orders for both TRD (Technocraft) and TRD USA Sports Parts.  TRD Sports Parts sold by TOYSPORT are sold as competition parts and there is limited or no warranty.
